The BD Day
One focused day. Three blocks. Done.
Week 1 of 13
Block 1 — Morning
90 minutes
Write & publish this week's anchor post. Reply to comments on last week's.

90 minutes

Block 3 — Afternoon
60 – 90 minutes
Pipeline review. Update tracker. Set next week's anchor post and outreach target. Capture diagnostic conversation notes.
